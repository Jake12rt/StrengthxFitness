Importance of Fitness

Fitness plays a crucial role in enhancing overall health and well-being, impacting both physical and mental aspects of life. Regular physical activity strengthens the cardiovascular system, improves muscular strength, and increases flexibility, which collectively contribute to better stamina and reduced risk of chronic diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and heart disease. Moreover, fitness is instrumental in maintaining a healthy weight and boosting immune function.

Benefit of Exercise

Exercise has been demonstrated to have good impacts on mental health. For example, a big study found that those who are sedentary have a 44% greater chance of developing depression. Another study found that just ninety minutes a week of exercise might provide results for people with mild to severe depression that were on par with those obtained with antidepressants. It appears that the secret is the release of feel-good, stress-relieving brain chemicals like serotonin and dopamine.

Fitness Levels & Goals

Beginner Level

Client Goals: General fitness or weight loss Preferences : Minimal equipment, at-home workout

Routine

  • Frequency: 3 days a week
  • Duration: 30-40 mins
  • Focus: Full-body workout
    Sample Routine
  • Warm-up (5 min): Brisk walking or marching in place
  • Strength (3 sets of 10 reps)
  • Bodyweight squats
  • Knee push-ups
  • Dumbbell or resistance band rows - Plank (hold for 20 seconds)
  • Cool-down (5 min): Stretching (hamstrings, quads, shoulders)

Intermidiate Level

Client Goals: Muscle gain and cardiovascular fitness Preferences : Gym workouts with weights

Routine

  • Frequency: 4 days a week
  • Duration: 45-60 mins
  • Focus: Split workout
    Sample Routine
  • Day 1: Lower Body Exercise
  • Day 2: Upper Body Exercise
  • Day 3: Core and Cardio
  • Day 4: Full-Body Exercise

Advance Level

Client Goals: Maximize performance and strength Preferences : High-intensity training, enjoys challenges

Routine

  • Frequency: 5-6 days a week
  • Duration: 60-90 mins
  • Focus: Strength workout
    Sample Routine
  • Day 1: Push Exercise
  • Day 2: Pull Exercise
  • Day 3: Legs Exercise
  • Day 4: Full-Body Exercise
  • Day 5: High-Intensity Workout
